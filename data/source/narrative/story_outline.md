**Game Style:** Top-down 2D Action RPG (Zelda-like)  
**Estimated Playtime:** 25+ Hours  
**Platform:** Windows & Linux (PC)

---

## 1. The Core Premise
The world of **Aethelgard** is a decaying landscape of "hollow" cities and silent ruins. The protagonist, **Kaelen**, is an immortal who once served as the world’s "Living Archive." A millennium ago, his mind was shattered, and his memories were scattered as physical shards. Without his mind, Kaelen has spent 1,000 years as a mindless wanderer. The game begins as he "awakens" with his first spark of consciousness.

---

## 2. The Backstory: The Shattering of the Aegis
### The Origin
Kaelen was created by the Old Gods to be the observer of time. He was incapable of forgetting, making him the ultimate judge of history. He was known as the **Aegis of Truth**.

### The Fall
**Malakor**, a brilliant but nihilistic sorcerer, believed that for a new world to be born, the old one must be entirely forgotten. Since Kaelen was immortal, Malakor couldn't kill him. Instead, he performed the **Oblivion Pulse**, a ritual that ripped Kaelen’s consciousness into **Seven Prismatic Shards**. 

* **The Result:** Aethelgard fell into a "Stagnant Age." History stopped being recorded, people lost their sense of purpose, and Malakor established the **Order of the Unwritten** to hunt down and destroy the Shards.

---

## 3. The Main Characters

### The Protagonist: Kaelen
* **Starting State:** A silent hermit in rags.
* **Evolution:** As he recovers shards, his appearance changes (his armor reforms) and his "Voice" returns (allowing for more complex dialogue options).

### The Antagonist: Malakor, The Unwritten
* **Goal:** To find the final "Shard of Choice" and erase it, effectively deleting the concept of "The Past" from human consciousness.
* **Presence:** He appears to Kaelen in "Memory Rifts" to taunt him with the painful parts of his past.

### Key Allies
* **Elara (The Historian):** A young woman from the village of Oakhaven who has studied the legends of the Aegis. She serves as your guide and "quest giver."
* **The Echoes:** Ghostly apparitions of people Kaelen knew 1,000 years ago. They appear in dungeons to provide clues and backstory.

---

## 4. Act Structure & Gameplay Flow

### Act I: The Awakening (Hours 1–6)
* **Location:** The Southern Wilds.
* **Objective:** Find the first three Shards to regain basic human functions.
* **Dungeon 1: The Sunken Library.** (Shard of Identity). Unlocks the ability to use a sword and read ancient signs.
* **Dungeon 2: The Whispering Woods.** (Shard of Empathy). Unlocks the "Compassion Pulse" (a tool to heal withered plants/paths).
* **Dungeon 3: The Iron Forge.** (Shard of Courage). Unlocks the "Dash" ability.
* **Act Climax:** Malakor burns Oakhaven. Kaelen realizes he cannot just hide; he must fight.

### Act II: The Burden of History (Hours 7–18)
* **Location:** The Great Central Highlands and Elemental Zones.
* **Objective:** Collect the four Elemental Shards.
* **The Twist:** Kaelen discovers that as the "Living Archive," he once condemned a whole civilization to exile to preserve "The Greater Good." He begins to doubt if he *wants* to remember everything.
* **Dungeon 4: The Frozen Spire.** (Shard of Logic). Unlocks the Ice Rod.
* **Dungeon 5: The Sun-Bleached Desert.** (Shard of Patience). Unlocks the Grappling Hook.
* **Dungeon 6: The Shadow Cathedral.** (Shard of Sorrow). This is a heavy narrative dungeon where Kaelen faces his own past crimes.
* **Dungeon 7: The Sky Reach.** (Shard of Joy). Unlocks the "Cape of Flight" (Zelda-style glider/float).

### Act III: The Convergence (Hours 19–25)
* **Location:** The Citadel of Erasure (Floating above the world).
* **Objective:** Confront Malakor and reclaim the "Shard of Choice."
* **Final Boss:** A multi-phase fight against Malakor. 
    * *Phase 1:* Physical combat.
    * *Phase 2:* A mental battle where Malakor uses Kaelen's own recovered memories against him.
* **The Ending:** Kaelen must choose: Reclaim immortality and the pain of the past, or become a mortal man with a clean slate, letting the world forge its own new history.

---

## 5. Sidequests & World Building
To reach the 25-hour mark, the world must be dense:
* **Memory Echoes (Collectibles):** 50 small "Fragments" hidden in the world. Each one provides a 15-second visual flashback and increases Max MP.
* **The Town Reconstruction:** Use gold/materials gathered from dungeons to help Elara rebuild Oakhaven. This unlocks better shops and upgrades.
* **Trial of the Ancestors:** A 50-floor combat arena for endgame players to unlock the "Master Aegis Blade."

---

## 6. Upgrade Paths (The "Memory" System)
Instead of traditional XP, players spend **Essence of Thought** (dropped by enemies) at "Shrines of Reflection."
1.  **Combat Branch:** Increases sword damage and unlocks the "Whirlwind" and "Counter-Parry."
2.  **Agility Branch:** Decreases stamina cost for dashing and adds a "Double Dash."
3.  **Essence Branch:** Increases the power of elemental items (Ice Rod, Fire Rod, etc.).

---

## 7. Narrative Design Tips for Cursor
* **Environmental Storytelling:** Since it's a Zelda-like, use the map! A broken statue in Act I should be "Remembered" and fixed in Act II to reveal a secret.
* **Dialogue Portraits:** Use different portraits for Kaelen as he regains his memory—starting with a hooded, shadowed face and ending with a clear, determined warrior.
