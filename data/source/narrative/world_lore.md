## 1. The Primordial Era: The Weaver and the Archive
In the beginning, there was no "Time," only "The Loom." The Old Gods wove the world into existence, but they realized that without memory, the world would loop in an endless, meaningless present.

For ten thousand years, Aethelgard flourished. Kaelen sat upon the **Throne of Glass** in the center of the world. Kings and queens would travel to him to settle disputes, for Kaelen could recall the exact terms of a treaty signed five centuries prior. Magic was fueled by "Recall"—the ability to pull the energy of past events into the present.

## 3. The Great Betrayal: The Rise of Malakor
